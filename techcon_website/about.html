History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for local developers and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences globally. Each year, it gathers innovators from all over the world to share ideas and collaborate on future technologies.

Mission

Our mission is to empower technologists, foster innovation, and build a community where knowledge and creativity thrive. We strive to provide a platform that inspires and drives positive change in the tech industry.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Lovelace is recognized for her pioneering work in computer science and was a keynote speaker in 2018, inspiring many with her vision of technology as a creative force.

Mr. Alan Turing

Mr. Turing, a cryptography expert, spoke in 2019 about the importance of security in technology and his contributions continue to influence the field today.

Ms. Grace Hopper

Ms. Hopper, a programming pioneer, addressed the 2021 conference on software development and its impact on modern computing.
